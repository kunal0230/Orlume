Introduction to Orlume

Orlume is a professional-grade AI photo editor that runs entirely in your browser. Built with privacy in mind, all processing happens locally on your device using WebGL2 GPU acceleration.

No account required. Start editing immediately — just upload an image and go.

Key Features

  • 3D Relighting — Add dramatic lighting using AI depth estimation
  • Background Removal — Pixel-perfect cutouts in seconds
  • Professional Presets — 40+ cinematic looks across 6 categories
  • HSL Color Mixer — 8-channel color control like Lightroom
  • AI Upscaling — Increase resolution up to 4x
  • Magic Eraser — Remove objects with AI inpainting

Quick Start

Getting started with Orlume takes just a few seconds:

1. Open the Editor

Navigate to orlume.io/editor or click "Open Editor" from the homepage.

2. Upload an Image

You can upload images in three ways:

  • Drag and drop — Drop any image directly onto the canvas
  • Click to browse — Click the upload area to select a file
  • Paste from clipboard — Use Ctrl/Cmd + V to paste

Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, HEIC (up to 50MB)

3. Make Adjustments

Use the sidebar panels to access different editing tools. Changes are applied in real-time with GPU acceleration.

4. Export Your Work

Click the Export button or press Ctrl/Cmd + E to save your edited image.

Interface Overview

The Orlume editor is divided into three main areas:

The Canvas

The central workspace where your image is displayed. You can zoom with scroll wheel or pinch gestures, and pan by clicking and dragging.

Left Sidebar — Tools

Quick access to specialized tools like Liquify, Healing Brush, and selection tools.

Right Sidebar — Adjustments

All image adjustment controls organized into collapsible panels:

  • Basic — Exposure, contrast, highlights, shadows, etc.
  • Color — Temperature, tint, vibrance, saturation
  • HSL — Per-channel hue, saturation, luminance
  • Effects — Vignette, grain, clarity, dehaze
  • AI — 3D relighting, depth tools, background removal
  • Presets — Professional preset library

Keyboard Shortcuts

Speed up your workflow with these keyboard shortcuts:

General

  • Ctrl/Cmd + Z — Undo
  • Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Z — Redo
  • Ctrl/Cmd + E — Export
  • Ctrl/Cmd + 0 — Fit to screen
  • Ctrl/Cmd + 1 — Zoom to 100%
  • Space + Drag — Pan canvas

Tools

  • V — Selection tool
  • B — Brush tool
  • E — Eraser tool
  • J — Healing brush
  • L — Liquify tool

Basic Adjustments

The Basic panel provides fundamental image adjustments:

Exposure

Controls overall image brightness. Positive values brighten the image, negative values darken it.

Contrast

Adjusts the difference between light and dark areas. Higher contrast creates more dramatic images.

Highlights & Shadows

Recover detail in bright and dark areas independently. Essential for high-contrast scenes.

Whites & Blacks

Set the white and black points of your image for optimal tonal range.

Color Grading & HSL

Orlume offers professional-grade color controls similar to Adobe Lightroom.

Temperature & Tint

Adjust the color balance of your image. Temperature controls warm/cool (orange/blue), while Tint controls green/magenta.

HSL Color Mixer

The HSL panel gives you independent control over 8 color channels:

  • Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Aqua, Blue, Purple, Magenta

For each channel, you can adjust:

  • Hue — Shift the color toward adjacent hues
  • Saturation — Increase or decrease color intensity
  • Luminance — Brighten or darken specific colors

Professional Presets

Apply one-click cinematic looks with our library of 40+ professional presets.

Categories

  • Portrait — Soft skin tones, flattering looks
  • Landscape — Vibrant nature photography
  • Urban & Street — Gritty, contemporary feels
  • Creative — Bold, artistic effects
  • Black & White — Classic monochrome conversions
  • Sky & Golden Hour — Warm, atmospheric lighting

Intensity Control

Use the intensity slider to blend between your original image and the preset effect. This allows for subtle adjustments or full preset application.

3D Relighting

Our AI-powered relighting tool uses depth estimation to add realistic lighting effects to any photo.

How It Works

  1. The AI analyzes your image to create a depth map
  2. You position virtual light sources in 3D space
  3. Lighting is applied based on the estimated geometry

Light Types

  • Key Light — Main light source, usually positioned above and to the side
  • Fill Light — Softer light to reduce shadows
  • Rim Light — Backlight creating edge highlights
  • Ambient — Overall environmental lighting

Background Removal

Remove backgrounds with AI-powered precision in just one click.

Usage

  1. Open the AI panel in the right sidebar
  2. Click "Remove Background"
  3. Wait for AI processing (typically 1-3 seconds)
  4. Optionally refine edges with the brush tool

Tips for Best Results

  • Works best with clear subject-background separation
  • Hair and fine details are handled automatically
  • Use the refinement brush for tricky edges

AI Upscaling

Increase image resolution while preserving and enhancing details.

Scale Options

  • 2x — Double resolution
  • 4x — Quadruple resolution (Pro feature)

The AI reconstructs missing detail using trained neural networks, producing sharper results than traditional upscaling methods.

Magic Eraser

Remove unwanted objects from your photos with AI inpainting.

How to Use

  1. Select the Magic Eraser tool from the left toolbar
  2. Brush over the object you want to remove
  3. The AI will fill in the area with appropriate content

Works best for removing small to medium-sized objects against relatively uniform backgrounds.

Export Options

Save your edited images in various formats and resolutions.

Resolution

  • Original — Same as input image
  • 720p — Free tier maximum
  • 1080p — Full HD
  • 4K — Ultra HD (Pro feature)

Quality

Adjust JPEG/WebP compression quality from 70-100%. Higher values mean larger files but better quality.

File Formats

  • JPEG — Universal compatibility, good for photos
  • PNG — Lossless, supports transparency
  • WebP — Modern format, best compression
