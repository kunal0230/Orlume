Our Mission

Orlume is a free, open-source AI photo editor that runs entirely in your browser. We believe professional-grade creative tools should be accessible to everyone — without expensive subscriptions, software installations, or privacy concerns.

Built by a passionate community of developers and designers, Orlume combines cutting-edge machine learning with intuitive design. Whether you're a photographer, designer, or just want to enhance your photos — Orlume is here for you.

Our Values

Privacy First

Your photos are personal. All processing happens locally on your device. We never see, upload, or store your images.

Free Forever

Orlume is and will always be free. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no hidden costs. Open source under the MIT license.

Community Driven

Built by contributors from around the world. Anyone can suggest features, report bugs, or contribute code.

Built on Cutting-Edge Technology

Orlume leverages the latest in web technologies to deliver desktop-class performance in your browser:

  • WebGL2 & WebGPU — GPU-accelerated rendering for real-time preview
  • TensorFlow.js — State-of-the-art neural networks running locally
  • ONNX Runtime — Optimized inference for AI models
  • Web Workers — Background processing without UI blocking
