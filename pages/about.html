What we stand for

Privacy First

Your photos never leave your device. All AI processing happens locally in the browser. We never see, upload, or store your images.

Free Forever

No subscriptions, no paywalls, no hidden costs. Orlume is and will always be free. Open source under the MIT license.

Community Driven

Built by contributors worldwide. Anyone can suggest features, report bugs, or contribute code. Every contribution matters.
