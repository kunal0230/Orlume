Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to how we build Orlume.

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

At Orlume Vision Labs, we take your privacy seriously. Your photos are processed entirely on your device. We do not upload, store, or analyze your images.

Our Privacy-First Approach

Orlume uses WebGL2/WebGPU to process images in your browser. Your images never leave your device unless you explicitly use cloud AI features.

What We Collect

  • Account info (if you sign up): email address
  • Usage analytics: anonymized data on features used
  • Device info: browser type, screen size for optimization

What We DON'T Collect

  • Your photos or edited images
  • Image contents or metadata
  • Facial recognition data

Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption, secure storage, and follow industry best practices to protect any data we do collect.

Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data anytime. Contact us at orlumevisionlabs@gmail.com.
