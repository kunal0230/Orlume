Announcement December 1, 2025

Introducing Orlume: The Free AI Photo Editor That Changes Everything

Professional-grade AI tools that run entirely in your browser. No downloads, no sign-ups, just powerful editing.

Today, we're launching Orlume -- a free, professional-grade AI photo editor that runs entirely in your browser. No downloads, no sign-ups, no subscriptions. Just open the editor and start creating.

Why We Built Orlume

Photo editing shouldn't require expensive software subscriptions or powerful desktop computers. We built Orlume because we believe professional-quality editing tools should be accessible to everyone -- photographers, designers, content creators, and hobbyists alike.

Unlike traditional photo editors that require installations and regular updates, Orlume runs entirely in your web browser. Thanks to advances in WebGPU technology, we can now run sophisticated AI models directly on your device, delivering desktop-quality results without the desktop software.

What Makes Orlume Different

3D Relighting: Our Flagship Feature

Orlume's 3D relighting is unlike anything available in other browser-based editors. Using neural depth estimation, we analyze the 3D structure of your photos and let you add realistic lighting effects as if you had a virtual light source in the scene.

Add rim lights to portraits, create dramatic side lighting, or fill in shadows -- all with intuitive controls. The AI understands the geometry of faces, objects, and environments, ensuring the lighting looks natural and professional.

AI Background Removal

Our background removal is powered by state-of-the-art segmentation models. Whether you're working with portraits, products, or complex scenes with hair and fur, Orlume delivers pixel-perfect edges that rival professional masking.

Professional Color Grading

With our 8-channel HSL color mixer, you have Lightroom-level control over your images. Adjust hue, saturation, and luminance for individual color ranges. Apply one of our 40+ professional presets across 6 categories: Portrait, Landscape, Urban, Creative, Black & White, and Golden Hour.

Smart Upscaling

Increase your image resolution up to 4x while the AI reconstructs lost details. Perfect for enlarging old photos or preparing images for print.

100% Privacy-First

Here's something that sets Orlume apart: your photos never leave your device. All processing happens locally in your browser using your device's GPU. We don't see, store, or train on your images. Your privacy isn't just a feature -- it's a fundamental principle of how Orlume works.

Free Forever

Orlume's core features are completely free with no watermarks or limitations. We believe everyone deserves access to professional photo editing tools. While we'll introduce optional premium features in the future, the powerful editing capabilities you see today will always remain free.

Get Started

Ready to transform your photos? Open the Orlume Editor and start editing right now. No account required, no downloads needed -- just drag and drop your image and experience the future of photo editing.

We're just getting started. Follow us on GitHub and LinkedIn, and check our changelog for the latest updates and new features.
